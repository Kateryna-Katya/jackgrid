ABOUT GAME

Pumpkin Master takes place in a festively decorated Halloween setting filled with glowing pumpkins and hidden surprises.
I wanted to keep it super simple - you don't need instructions or anything, just start clicking and you'll figure it out, it's pretty simple - just click around and see what you find

Each pumpkin can reveal rewards, visual effects, or special events that change the pace of play. With pumpkins appearing endlessly, the experience encourages exploration and keeps things interesting so you don't get bored.

The Game

At the center of Pumpkin Master is a field of interactive pumpkins.When you click a pumpkin, you get these cool little animations and spooky sounds - nothing too scary though, just fun Halloween vibes.

Every now and then you'll hit a special pumpkin that triggers this mini bonus round - gets your heart racing a bit before settling back into the regular rhythm.
